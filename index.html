Wheaton Art Alumna Jessina Leonard ’14 Awarded Fulbright Scholarship

Congratulations to Wheaton College alumna Jessina Leonard ’14 who was awarded a Fulbright Scholarship. The award will allow Leonard to spend 10 months in Germany researching the lives of three medieval mystics and working on her own photography, which is informed by her research.

HoneyRock Hosts Fatherless Camp Ministry Summit
